Runway Gen-4 / Gen-4 Turbo prompt guidelines:

BEST AT: cinematic camera moves, smooth transitions, photorealistic scenes, longer-form coherence (10-second shots stay consistent). Highest prompt adherence among Western video models.

WEAK AT: in-frame text (no model is great here, Runway worst), specific named faces (copyright filter), audio (no native audio — generate silent and add sound separately).

VERSIONS:

- Gen-4 — flagship, slower (~60-90s), best quality. Use for hero shots.
- Gen-4 Turbo — ~3× faster, slightly softer detail. Use for bulk B-roll.

PROMPT PATTERN (≤50 words, single moment per shot):

```
[Subject + action], [camera move], [lighting + atmosphere], [style/grade]
```

CAMERA MOVE VOCABULARY (Runway responds well — pick ONE):

- `slow push-in` — builds emphasis
- `slow pull-back` — reveals environment
- `dolly left/right` — lateral tracking
- `pan left/right` — rotation around camera axis
- `tilt up/down` — vertical pivot
- `orbit 45 degrees clockwise` — 3D feel for products
- `handheld subtle drift` — documentary
- `locked-off tripod` — intentional stillness

EXAMPLES:

Product shot:

```
A glass perfume bottle on dark marble, slow orbit 45 degrees clockwise, single overhead key light, hard shadow below, neutral cool grade, 9:16
```

Lifestyle B-roll:

```
A barista pulling an espresso shot at a cafe counter, slow push-in toward hands, warm window light from camera-left, muted desaturated grade
```

Hero opener:

```
A neon sign reading reflects on wet asphalt, slow tilt up to reveal city skyline at dusk, cool blue ambient with magenta practical lights, anamorphic look
```

AVOID:

- People's faces (especially named/famous — triggers copyright filter)
- In-frame readable text (renders garbled)
- Brand logos (will not reproduce)
- Two simultaneous camera moves ("push-in AND pan" — pick one)

ASPECT RATIOS: 16:9 (best quality), 9:16 (good but slightly weaker motion coherence), 1:1, 4:3. For reels, 9:16 works but expect 1-2 extra retries vs 16:9.

DURATION: 5s or 10s clips.

COST NOTE: Gen-4 ~$0.50-1.00/clip, Turbo ~$0.20. Use Turbo for bulk, Gen-4 for the 1-2 hero shots that carry the reel.
